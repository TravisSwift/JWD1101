3_4 Lab Starter Multi Page Website

    Assignment Requirements:

  • Welcome page/home screen

  • h1 element with the name of your site

  • A p element with a paragraph about you.

  • ul elements with a list of hyperlinks to the other 3 pages. The links should be labeled Video, Tables, and Images

  • Embed a video of your choice from youtube in an iframe and include hyperlinks back to your home page.

  • Create a table that displays the information below. Include hyperlinks back to your home page.

  • Insert three images with figure and img tags. Include a figcaption for each image and include hyperlinks back to your home page.
