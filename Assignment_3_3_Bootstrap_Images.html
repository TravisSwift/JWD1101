Outdoor Kids

By Danielle Cohen from childmind.org

These days, kids spend much more time inside, mostly thanks to technology. The average American child spends about 4 to 7 minutes a day playing outside and over 7 hours a day in front of a screen. Spending time outdoors isn’t just enjoyable — it’s also necessary. Many researchers agree that kids who play outside are smarter, happier, better at paying attention and less anxious than kids who spend more time indoors.
